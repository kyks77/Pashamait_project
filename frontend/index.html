What is novo?

novo is a document generator built specifically for freelance developers, designers, and creative professionals who work with remote clients. Instead of digging through bloated Word templates or paying hundreds for a lawyer on a small project, you fill in a guided form and download a clean PDF — contract, invoice, or proposal — tailored to your project.

We focus on the clauses that actually matter for freelance work: payment schedules, revision limits, IP ownership, confidentiality, termination, and scope changes. You choose the options; novo assembles the language.
